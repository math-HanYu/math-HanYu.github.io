    Brief self-introduction

    ⛄ CV

    I am a Zeeman Lecturer at the University of Warwick. I was a research associate at DPMMS, University of Cambridge and a research fellow at Corpus Christi College. I was a PhD student at the university of St. Andrews, supervised by Dr Jonathan Fraser and Dr Mike Todd

    I'm interested in Number Theory, Fractal Geometry, Dynamical Systems, Ergodic Theory, Probability Theory, Additive Combinatorics, Harmonic Analysis.
